About the App

The Quran Player is an interactive audio player designed to provide users with an easy way to listen to the Quran. It features a user-friendly interface, allowing users to navigate through different Surahs, control playback, and adjust volume settings.

Features

  • Play, pause, and navigate through Surahs.
  • Volume control with mute functionality.
  • Dynamic display of the currently playing Surah.
  • Attractive UI with smooth animations.
